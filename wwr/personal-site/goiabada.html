Goiabada

Goiabada, an iconic sweet from Brazil, traces its origins to the tropical guava fruit. Since colonial times, the combination of guava pulp and sugar has created this bittersweet delight that has become a staple on Brazilian tables. Good Candy brings a special approach to this tradition. We carefully select the finest guavas and skillfully craft pieces of goiabada that preserve Brazilian authenticity while adding a unique touch. Each piece of Good Candy's goiabada is more than just a sweet; it's a tribute to Brazil's rich culinary heritage. When you open a package, you're invited to savor a piece of the sweet tradition that connects us. Come and enjoy Good Candy, where goiabada comes to life, staying true to its roots but with a touch of innovation.

Good Candy; 2023 - Kelvyn Lima
