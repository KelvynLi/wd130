Rapadura

Rapadura: A Sweet Symphony of Sugarcane and Tradition Rapadura, a sweet treat deeply rooted in Brazilian culture, is a simple yet flavorful creation made from sugarcane. Its history intertwines with the vast sugarcane fields that have been a part of Brazil's landscape for centuries. Crafted by boiling sugarcane juice until it thickens and solidifies, rapadura captures the essence of sweetness in a compact form. Its origins trace back to indigenous communities and Portuguese settlers who embraced the abundance of sugarcane in the region. At Good Candy, we honor this tradition by carefully selecting the finest sugarcane and using time-honored techniques to create rapadura with a touch of modern finesse. Each bite tells a story of the sun-kissed fields and the hands that have perfected this culinary art over generations. As you unwrap a piece of Good Candy's rapadura, you're not just enjoying a sweet treat; you're experiencing a delicious piece of Brazilian history. Join us at Good Candy, where rapadura embodies the sweet harmony of sugarcane and tradition.

Good Candy; 2023 - Kelvyn Lima
