Beijinho and brigadeiro

In the 1940s, Brazil witnessed the birth of two sweet treasures: brigadeiros and beijinhos. The brigadeiro, emerging as a sweet solace in challenging times, won hearts with its perfect blend of condensed milk, chocolate, and butter. Meanwhile, the beijinho, delicate and tropical, captivated palates with its smooth fusion of condensed milk and shredded coconut. These confectionery classics, wrapped in memories of parties and celebrations, found a new expression in Good Candy. Founded by lovers of sweetness and committed to merging tradition and innovation, Good Candy transformed brigadeiros and beijinhos into true masterpieces. In every Good Candy store, customers are invited on a sensory journey. The master confectioners at Good Candy, dedicated to authenticity, blend artisanal techniques with premium ingredients, resulting in brigadeiros and beijinhos that transcend expectations. Good Candy is not just a candy brand; it is a celebration of sweetness that marks eras. Each sweet is a story, a tradition kept alive with a modern touch. When savoring a brigadeiro or beijinho from Good Candy, you not only taste an exceptional treat but also participate in a sweet narrative that bridges past, present, and future.At Good Candy, each sweet is an affirmation of our passion for sweetness, celebrating the tradition that inspires us and the innovation that propels us forward. Come be a part of this sweet story at Good Candy, where each bite is a unique experience.

Good Candy; 2023 - Kelvyn Lima
